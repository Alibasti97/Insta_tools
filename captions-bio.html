Understanding Instagram Bio & Caption Limits

Instagram restricts bios to 150 characters and captions to 2,200 characters. If you go beyond the limit, your text may get cut off or fail to publish correctly. This free counter helps you stay compliant, avoid errors, and create engaging content.

Best Practices for Writing Instagram Bios

  • Keep it short and brand-focused.
  • Include a call-to-action (CTA) like “Follow for tips.”
  • Use emojis to add personality without wasting characters.
  • Add a link-in-bio to drive traffic.

Best Practices for Writing Instagram Captions

  • Use the first 125 characters wisely (that’s what shows before “See More”).
  • Include relevant hashtags but don’t overstuff.
  • Write engaging stories, tips, or hooks to connect with your audience.
  • Keep captions easy to read with line breaks and emojis.
